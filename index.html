The contrast-rich natural surroundings, the coming together of Alpine freshness and Mediterranean charm, the keeping alive of ancient traditions and customs and the reliability and spontaneity of the people make South Tyrol truly special. The town of Merano, the surrounding villages and the Vinschgau Valley are unique. The region, which is so varied and rich in contrasts, surprises us time and again. Scaling mountains, experiencing the enchanting views, exploring the diverse cultural landscape and enjoying the delicious South Tyrolean cuisine - all of these things are enhanced by a quality glass of our Merano wines.

We are 100% passionate about our wines, so immerse yourself in our world and join us on a special journey of taste discovery.
